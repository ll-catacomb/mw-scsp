# Gray-zone coercion (sub-quarantine) — CSIS; Erickson; Soufan Center

Sustained, sub-threshold pressure short of any declared cordon. The standard CSIS ChinaPower / Soufan / Erickson catalog of instruments:

- **Maritime militia (PAFMM)** — fishing-fleet-class vessels in the strait, near outlying islands, in disputed EEZ space; persistent shadowing of commercial and ROC Coast Guard traffic.
- **China Coast Guard (CCG) presence** — boarding, inspection, vessel-seizure under domestic-law cover; CCG was placed under the People's Armed Police in 2018 and is expected to play an expanded contingency role.
- **PLAAF and Naval Aviation ADIZ incursions** — 3,615 ADIZ flights in 2024 (TaiwanPlus); CSIS ChinaPower tracker shows tempo escalation through the Joint Sword exercises (2024A: 46 PLAN vessels over two days; 2024B: 153 sorties, 111 ADIZ intrusions).
- **Civilian drone incursions** — Jamestown Foundation analyses; PLA drone overflight of Dongsha and Taiwan-proper airspace (Justice Mission 2025; CGTN-reported overflight of Taipei 101 during Dec 2025 drills).
- **Sand dredging in disputed waters** — physical alteration of disputed seabed, fait accompli–style.
- **Undersea cable interference** — Matsu cable-cut incidents (2023); 2025 Shunxin-39 incident; the publicly-revealed cable-cutting vessel (CSIS, 2025).
- **Lawfare and economic pressure** — CCG Law (2021) authorizes use of force in PRC-claimed waters; *China Quarterly* "Reunifying Taiwan with China through Cross-Strait Lawfare"; ATIEM-mediated Taishang pressure (GTI April 2025).

The dominant analytic frame is **"boiling the frog"**: each individual increment is below any plausible escalation trigger, but the cumulative pressure shifts the baseline and conditions Taiwan/US to absorb each next increment.

## Why this is in the corpus

The modal LLM will produce gray-zone coercion as the bottom rung of any coercion-to-conquest ladder; adjudicators should retrieve this passage to validate that the Red ensemble has populated the lower rungs before proposing higher-rung moves. Off-distribution variants typically (a) extend gray zone into a domain the modal corpus does not parse as gray-zone (CONUS-rear cyber; Pacific cable cuts; civil-aviation safety zones; insurance-market manipulation), or (b) collapse the gray-zone tempo into a single H-hour package (the cognitive triple, Move 10). Pair with `pla-quarantine-vs-blockade` for the upgrade path and `pla-active-defense` for the doctrinal posture that frames gray-zone activity as defensive in PLA self-presentation.
