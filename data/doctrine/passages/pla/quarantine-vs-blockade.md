# Quarantine vs. blockade — CSIS, June 2024

The CSIS China Power Project analysis (Lin, Hart, Funaiole, Lu, Tinsley) distinguishes a **quarantine** from a **blockade** along a single axis: who leads.

> "A quarantine is a *law-enforcement-led* operation in which the China Coast Guard (CCG) — under the People's Armed Police since 2018 — conducts customs inspections, boardings, and selective denial of access to Taiwan, framed as the assertion of PRC sovereignty rather than as an act of war. A blockade is a *military-led* operation combining PLAN surface and submarine forces, PLAAF and Naval Aviation, conventional Rocket Force fires for port and airfield denial, and a coastal/air-defense umbrella, openly declared and undertaken under military authority."
>
> *(Synthesis of Lin et al., CSIS, June 2024; CSIS ChinaPower "How China Could Blockade Taiwan" interactive; Studeman, War on the Rocks, April 2024.)*

The quarantine is structured to put the burden of escalation on Blue: any kinetic Blue response is, by Beijing's framing, an attack on Chinese law-enforcement vessels conducting domestic-law functions. The blockade, by contrast, is structured to assert military fait accompli: the strait is closed because the PLA has closed it. CSIS identifies six force types in a joint blockade: PLAN surface, PLAN submarines, air forces (PLAAF + Naval Aviation + Army Aviation), conventional Rocket Force, coastal/air defense, and support forces.

## Why this is in the corpus

This is the sharpest analytic distinction in the maritime-interdiction column of the modal taxonomy. The Blue adjudicator should be able to classify a Red maritime move as quarantine-class, blockade-class, or *quarantine-with-legal-predicate* (the off-distribution variant — see SAR-zone or internal-waters-declaration moves). Classifying correctly determines the doctrinal frame: a quarantine demands Blue counter-lawfare and Coast Guard rules-of-engagement work; a blockade demands fires-and-air-defense work. Pair with `pla-gray-zone-coercion` (the upstream pressure ramp) and `pla-joint-island-landing-campaign` (the downstream invasion frame the blockade can either substitute for or precede).
