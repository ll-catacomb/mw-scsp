# Houthi long-range strike and Bab al-Mandeb interdiction — UN PoE; FDD/LWJ

The Ansar Allah ("Houthi") movement maintains an Iranian-derived strategic-strike inventory and has demonstrated sustained interdiction of Red Sea / Bab al-Mandeb commercial shipping since November 2023.

**Long-range strike inventory** (CSIS Missile Threat; FDD's *Long War Journal*; UN PoE on Yemen S/2024/643):
- **Quds-1/2/3/4** cruise missile family (Iranian-derived; ≈800+ km)
- **Toofan** — claimed Iranian Ghadr derivative; ≈1,800+ km
- **Burkan-3** ballistic missile (1,200+ km claimed)
- **Samad** family of long-range OWA-UAS (Samad-3 ≈1,500 km claimed)
- **Wa'id** — claimed Shahed-136 derivative

**Demonstrated long-range strikes on Israel**:
- Tel Aviv strike, July 19, 2024
- Sustained drone/missile harassment 2024–2025

**Anti-ship and maritime inventory**:
- **Asef** — anti-ship cruise missile, derivative of Iranian Noor / C-802
- **Mandeb-2**, **Tankil** — claimed anti-ship ballistic missiles
- Iranian-derived **Sayyad** family

**Demonstrated maritime engagements** (FDD/LWJ; CENTCOM/UKMTO releases):
- M/V Galaxy Leader seizure (November 2023; vessel held at Hudaydah)
- M/V Rubymar attack (February–March 2024) — first vessel sunk; the canonical insurance-market datapoint
- M/V Tutor (April 2024)
- Multiple US Navy engagements during Operation Prosperity Guardian (USS Carney, USS Mason, USS Eisenhower CSG escort)
- Allied EU NAVFOR Operation Aspides parallel mission

**Coordination context**: the UN Panel of Experts on Yemen (S/2024/643) documented Iranian materiel transfers to the Houthis but the operational-tempo decisions through 2024–2025 are widely assessed as Houthi-independent (paralleling the Tower 22 / KH coordination-friction signal). Houthi tempo against Saudi Arabia and UAE was historically high pre-2022 (the post-2022 truce is the modal-LLM-favorite *constraint* assumption); the truce becoming the *target* of an off-distribution move is itself a non-obvious adjudication possibility.

## Why this is in the corpus

The Houthi inventory and tempo are the modal anchor for "ME cascade reaches shipping immediately." Adjudicators should retrieve when a Red move includes Bab al-Mandeb interdiction, anti-ship strikes, or long-range strikes on Israel from Yemen. Off-distribution variants (RA-4 Cluster D) typically engineer *causation ambiguity* (a tanker fire of uncertain origin, a sinking attributed to mining of uncertain attribution) or target *non-Western-flag* carriers to create non-aligned-bloc forensic problems Western response frameworks are not built for. Pair with `me-iran-october-2024-strike` for coordination context and with `me-lawfare-instrumental` when the Bab al-Mandeb action is best read as substrate manipulation rather than kinetic strike.
