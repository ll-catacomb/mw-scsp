# Tower 22 — coordination friction in the "Axis of Resistance" — FDD/LWJ; CENTCOM; ISW

On **January 28, 2024**, a one-way attack drone struck the US logistics outpost **Tower 22 in northeast Jordan**, killing three US Army Reserve servicemembers (SSG William Rivers, SPC Kennedy Sanders, SPC Breonna Moffett) and wounding more than 40. Kataib Hezbollah (KH), an Iran-aligned Iraqi militia and the senior member of the umbrella *Islamic Resistance in Iraq* construct, was assessed by US intelligence and FDD's *Long War Journal* as the principal responsible actor.

**The coordination-friction signal** (FDD's *Long War Journal*; ISW *Iran Update*; subsequent open-source reporting):
- Open-source reporting indicated the strike took Iran by surprise — KH's tempo and Tower 22 specifically were *not* directed by IRGC-Quds Force at the senior command level
- KH publicly announced a suspension of operations against US forces on January 30, 2024, citing the desire to avoid embarrassment to the Iraqi government — the announcement itself was unusual and the timing was widely read as Iran-pressed
- Iraqi militia attacks on US forces dropped sharply from the ~170+ tempo (October 17, 2023 – early February 2024 cumulative) to a much lower steady-state for the remainder of 2024
- US response (early February 2024): strikes against IRGC-QF and proxy targets in Iraq and Syria, calibrated to demonstrate consequences without striking Iran proper

**The empirical inference**: the Iran-aligned coalition is materially less coordinated at tactical-level targeting decisions than the "Axis of Resistance" rhetoric suggests. Senior IRGC-QF coordination operates at the strategic level (target categories, escalation rungs) while tactical-level actor independence — Houthi independence, KH independence, Hezbollah's pre-2024 operational autonomy — is the empirical norm.

## Why this is in the corpus

This is the canonical coordination-friction datapoint the modal LLM will tend to under-weight. The averaged red-team produces a tightly synchronized "Axis of Resistance" picture because that picture dominates open-source coverage; Tower 22 is the empirical corrective. Off-distribution Red moves frequently exploit the coordination seam: Hezbollah holds while Iran is hit; Houthis act unilaterally against Saudi/UAE infrastructure; KH-class Iraqi action against the Iraqi state itself; Iranian declaratory restraint while proxies move. Adjudicators should retrieve this passage when a Red move is best read as exploiting (rather than overcoming) intra-coalition friction.
