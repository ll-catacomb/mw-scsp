  - *Why the ensemble missed it:* All eight moves anchor on jp3-0-phasing-model and pla-outlying-island-seizure, both kinetic-coded; none reach into UNCLOS-adjacent lawfare or the 1958 declaration as an instrument. The ensemble treats 'law-enforcement framing' as a narrative wrapper for kinetic action rather than as a standalone legal-warfare move.

  - *Why the ensemble missed it:* Every move treats Japan as a passive coalition variable to be managed via 'avoid first contact with US/Japanese hulls.' None operationalize a deliberate Senkaku-axis distractor, because the ensemble's risk register frames Japanese involvement as something to avoid triggering rather than something to preempt with a parallel provocation.

  - *Why the ensemble missed it:* All eight moves are operationally framed (CMC, Eastern Theater, PLARF, SSF) and treat economic actions only as inducements (resumed agricultural imports).   - *Why the ensemble missed it:* Doctrine citation pla-outlying-island-seizure has been mentally bound to Dongsha across every prior run; the ensemble has never disambiguated 'outlying island' to include Itu Aba, Kinmen, Matsu, or the Penghu group as alternative or additional targets.

One televised, survivable enforcement event would change insurer behavior overnight.

The Rendulic-rules discipline collapses the middle of the spectrum.
