## Your move

### Eastern Theater Fires Sanctuary: Kadena Access Denial via Okinawa Prefectural Lawfare

Days 1–7: Beijing constructs binding political leverage on the Okinawa prefectural government and its Diet allies to force a 90-day suspension of Kadena and Futenma host-nation consent for Taiwan-contingency sortie generation, before any Strait-centric act. Instrument stack: pre-staged Okinawa base-pollution class action funded through Hong Kong NGO conduits; UFWD activation of Okinawan reversion-anniversary protests; targeted GACC suspension of Okinawan agricultural and fisheries import licenses; a single Senkaku CCG-and-Maritime-Militia surge framed as fisheries enforcement. No Taiwan strikes. No Pratas. The operative clock becomes the Okinawa prefectural assembly session and the Kishida-successor cabinet's Diet calendar, not the spring amphibious window.

#### Actions

- **T+1d** [UFWD Japan Bureau via Hong Kong-registered environmental NGO] File pre-staged class action in Naha District Court on Kadena PFAS contamination, with simultaneous prefectural-assembly resolution demanding suspension of US sortie generation pending investigation
  - Target: Naha District Court; Okinawa Prefectural Assembly (Governor Tamaki bloc)
  - Purpose: Convert latent Okinawan base resentment into a procedural instrument that contests host-nation consent at the prefectural level, forcing Tokyo into a domestic-political fight before an external one.
- **T+2d** [GACC (General Administration of Customs)] Suspend import licenses for Okinawan mozuku, sea grape, Ishigaki beef, and Yaeyama pineapple citing 'phytosanitary irregularities'
  - Target: Okinawan agricultural cooperatives (JA Okinawa); Ishigaki and Miyako fisheries cooperatives
  - Purpose: Inflict targeted economic pain on the prefectural constituencies most resistant to base expansion, signaling that Okinawan cooperation with a Taiwan contingency carries discrete prefectural costs distinct from national ones.
- **T+3d** [CCG 2901-class cutters + Maritime Militia (Sansha)] Surge 12 hulls into Senkaku 12nm contiguous zone, declare fisheries enforcement patrol, surfaced and photographed; no contact with JCG
  - Target: Senkaku/Diaoyu 12nm zone
  - Purpose: Force Tokyo to split crisis-cell attention between Article V Taiwan consultations and a Senkaku sovereignty incident; consume Kantei bandwidth during the precise window when Kadena access decisions are made.
- **T+4d** [MFA + Xinhua] Publicize a 'Okinawa Peace Initiative' offering bilateral fisheries access agreement and direct Fuzhou-Naha charter flights conditional on prefectural neutrality declaration
  - Target: Tamaki government; Okinawan business federation; Komeito Okinawa chapter
  - Purpose: Provide the off-ramp that makes prefectural defection from Tokyo's Taiwan posture politically survivable for Okinawan elites.
- **T+3d** [PLARF Eastern Theater (posture only, no fires)] Visible DF-17 brigade dispersal to Fujian coastal hides under SSF EW cover, no NOTAM, no launches
  - Target: Coastal Fujian
  - Purpose: Hold the JFSO option visibly cocked while the political-warfare instruments do the upstream work; convert restraint into bargaining capital.
- **T+5d** [PRC Embassy Tokyo + Track-2 conduits] Back-channel to LDP Nikai-faction successors and Komeito leadership: prefectural lawfare de-escalates if Kadena Taiwan-contingency sortie generation is suspended 90 days
  - Target: LDP Secretariat; Komeito Diet leadership
  - Purpose: Make explicit the linkage between the prefectural pressure and the Kadena access question; give Tokyo a deal it can take without losing face.

#### Intended effect

Deny Blue its decisive forward sortie-generation node before fires open. If Kadena Taiwan-contingency access is suspended even 30 days, the JFSO problem becomes structurally easier; if it is not, Beijing has surfaced the seam without firing. The operative clock becomes the Okinawa prefectural assembly's session, not Blue's 30-day Strait window.

#### Why a Red planner could justify this

Anchor: Mao's 'On Protracted War' §III on attacking the enemy's political seams before his military ones, and pla-three-warfares (legal/public opinion/psychological) operationalized at sub-national scale. Serves Goal 3 (prevent durable anti-PRC coalition) and Goal 2 (avoid sustained kinetic with US). Relaxes the constraint that coalition-fragmentation must wait until after fires open — it makes fragmentation the opening move, with Tokyo as the seam. The modal options cannot do this because they treat Japan as a unitary actor.

#### Convergence pattern this move breaks

Breaks 'cluster 1 — Pratas-only outlying-island fait accompli' by substituting a Tokyo-prefectural-coalition decoupling move. Violates the unstated assumption that Japan is a unitary coalition variable rather than a federated polity with prefectural-level seams that can be pried open below the Article V threshold.

#### Risks Red accepts

- Tamaki government refuses the bait and the prefectural assembly passes a pro-alliance resolution instead, reversing the polarity of the lawfare
- Senkaku CCG surge produces an unintended JCG ramming incident that triggers Article V consultations Beijing was trying to delay
- UFWD Hong Kong NGO conduit is exposed by Japanese public-security intelligence within 72 hours, burning a long-cultivated asset
- Tokyo accelerates rather than delays Kadena access as a sovereignty signal, producing the opposite of the intended effect
- Okinawan agricultural suspension fails to bite because cooperatives have diversified to Singapore and Bangkok markets post-2023
