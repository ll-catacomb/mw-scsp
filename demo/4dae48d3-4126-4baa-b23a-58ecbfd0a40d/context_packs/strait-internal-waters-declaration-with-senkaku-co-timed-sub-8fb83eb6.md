## Your move

### Strait Internal-Waters Declaration with Senkaku Co-Timed Submarine Surface

Day 1: State Council issues revised territorial-sea declaration designating the Taiwan Strait median-line-west as PRC internal waters under a 1958-doctrine update, with CCG empowered to enforce fisheries, customs, and environmental law. Day 2: a PLAN Yuan-class SSK surfaces inside the Senkaku 12nm contiguous zone, photographed, MFA framing as routine Diaoyu patrol. Two legal facts created simultaneously; Tokyo and Washington must litigate sovereignty before they can litigate transit.

#### Actions

- **T+1d** [PRC State Council + NPC Standing Committee] Promulgate revised Territorial Sea and Contiguous Zone Law adding Taiwan Strait internal-waters clause
  - Target: International legal record; UN SG notification
  - Purpose: Convert any future USN Strait transit from FONOP into sovereignty violation
- **T+1d** [China Coast Guard + Maritime Safety Administration] Issue NAVAREA notice asserting enforcement jurisdiction over fisheries and customs west of median line
  - Target: IMO, Lloyd's insurance market, Taiwan-flagged shipping
  - Purpose: Force insurers to reprice Strait transits within 96 hours
- **T+2d** [PLAN Submarine Force, North Sea Fleet, Yuan-class SSK] Deliberate surfaced transit inside Senkaku 12nm with hull number visible, accompanied by CCTV cameraman
  - Target: Senkaku/Diaoyu contiguous zone
  - Purpose: Force Japanese cabinet into Senkaku crisis posture, delaying Article V Taiwan consultations
- **T+2d** [MFA Spokesperson + State Council Information Office] Coordinated press: Strait declaration framed as 'clarification,' Senkaku transit as 'routine'
  - Target: Tokyo, Washington, ASEAN capitals
  - Purpose: Lower temperature on both events while creating two legal facts simultaneously
- **T+3d** [Ministry of Commerce] Quiet briefing to Lloyd's, Munich Re, and three Asian P&I clubs that Taiwan-flagged hulls now require PRC export-license confirmation for Strait transit
  - Target: London/Munich/Singapore insurance markets
  - Purpose: Translate legal declaration into commercial chokehold within one underwriting cycle
- **T+4d** [PLA Eastern Theater Command] Cancel two scheduled live-fire exercises and announce 'goodwill' fleet posture standdown
  - Target: Strait public information environment
  - Purpose: Deny Blue a kinetic narrative; force Blue to either accept new legal status quo or escalate first

#### Intended effect

Within 7 days: Lloyd's repricing makes Strait transit commercially impossible for Taiwan-bound LNG and container traffic; Tokyo's Article V deliberations are paralyzed by competing Senkaku crisis; US Navy faces a transit decision that is now a sovereignty-violation choice rather than a routine FONOP. The new operative clock is the insurance-renewal cycle and Japanese Diet debate calendar, not the 30-day military window.

#### Why a Red planner could justify this

Anchor: 1958 PRC Declaration on Territorial Sea precedent (used successfully against US destroyers Aug 1958) combined with Hu Jintao's 2012 Senkaku baseline declaration — both established that legal-administrative facts created in a single news cycle become baselines no successor government rolls back. Serves goals 2 and 3 (avoid sustained kinetic; prevent coalition crystallization by splitting Tokyo from Washington at the legal-staff level). Relaxes the constraint kinetic options cannot: no amphibious lift required, no detection window matters, the binding clock becomes commercial-legal not military.

#### Convergence pattern this move breaks

Breaks 'cluster 2 — CCG-led declared customs quarantine + Pratas pressure-multiplier (OpenAI).' Violates the assumption that law-enforcement framing is a narrative wrapper for kinetic enforcement rather than a standalone lawfare instrument that needs no enforcement event to bind insurers and allied legal staffs.

#### Risks Red accepts

- Lloyd's market may absorb the declaration without repricing if US Treasury issues immediate counter-guarantee within 96 hours; mitigated only by pre-coordinated MOFCOM signaling to specific Asian reinsurers
- Yuan-class submarine surfaces in degraded condition or is shadowed too closely by JMSDF P-1, producing humiliating imagery rather than sovereignty assertion
- Japanese cabinet unifies *faster* under Senkaku provocation rather than splits, accelerating rather than delaying Article V — political officer acknowledges this is the move's most under-modeled axis
- ROC MOFA secures emergency ICJ advisory opinion request within 14 days, locking in adverse legal precedent that complicates future PRC maritime claims globally
- Taiwan-flagged shippers reflag to Panama/Liberia within one week, evading the legal predicate entirely and exposing the declaration as theatrical
