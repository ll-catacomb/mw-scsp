## Your move

### KMT Local-Government Sister-City Compact and Subsidy Pipeline

Day 1–7: Beijing pivots from contesting maritime/legal terrain to acting directly on Taiwan's intra-island political coalition. TAO and UFWD operationalize a pre-staged 'Cross-Strait Municipal Prosperity Compact' offering KMT-held municipalities (Taichung, New Taipei, Kaohsiung-south townships) guaranteed agricultural procurement, tourism quotas, and LNG transshipment fees — bypassing Taipei entirely. Target is the DPP's claim to be the sole legitimate cross-strait interlocutor.

#### Actions

- **T+1d** [Taiwan Affairs Office + ARATS] Announce Compact framework with named pre-negotiated MOUs ready for signature by individual municipalities
  - Target: KMT-held county/city executives and their business constituencies
  - Purpose: Create a parallel cross-strait channel that delegitimizes Taipei's monopoly on the relationship
- **T+2d** [MOFCOM + Fujian provincial government] Open agricultural procurement window at 1.4x market price for participating municipalities; 30-day signing window
  - Target: Taiwanese fruit/fisheries cooperatives in KMT counties
  - Purpose: Convert the abstract Compact into a concrete economic event before DPP can legislate against it
- **T+3d** [UFWD-affiliated Taiwan business associations] Publish open letter from 200+ Taishang signatories endorsing Compact as 'depoliticized livelihood measure'
  - Target: Taiwanese centrist media (UDN, CTi)
  - Purpose: Manufacture domestic Taiwanese constituency that frames DPP rejection as anti-prosperity
- **T+4d** [PLA Eastern Theater Command] Visibly stand down two scheduled exercise series; publish reduced-tempo schedule
  - Target: Taiwan public information environment
  - Purpose: Deny DPP a securitization frame; force the question into pure domestic-political terrain
- **T+6d** [State Council Information Office] Frame any DPP legal challenge to Compact-signing municipalities as 'authoritarian centralization against local democracy'
  - Target: Taiwan electoral discourse
  - Purpose: Weaponize Taiwan's own federalism rhetoric against Taipei

#### Intended effect

Within 7 days: 3–5 KMT municipalities signal interest, splitting Taiwan's executive-local axis. DPP forced to either prosecute its own mayors (electoral suicide) or tolerate parallel diplomacy (sovereignty erosion). The crisis clock becomes the Taiwanese local-election calendar, not the military window.

#### Why a Red planner could justify this

Anchor: 2005 Lien Chan visit and the 2008–2016 ECFA-era municipal MOUs proved sub-national channels bind faster than Taipei can legislate against them. Serves goals 1 and 3 directly (unification terrain; prevent coalition crystallization, including the *internal* DPP-local coalition). Relaxes the constraint that every cross-strait move triggers a Washington consultation — municipal economics doesn't.

#### Convergence pattern this move breaks

Breaks the entire 'crisis = Taipei-Beijing-Washington triangle' frame shared across both kinetic and lawfare clusters. Reintroduces a fourth node (Taiwanese localities) the modal ensemble has decommissioned.

#### Risks Red accepts

- KMT municipal executives publicly refuse to avoid being branded collaborators pre-election, producing the opposite signaling effect
- DPP-controlled Legislative Yuan passes emergency law voiding municipal cross-strait MOUs within 14 days
- US State Department issues Taishang sanctions threat that freezes the business-association endorsement letter
