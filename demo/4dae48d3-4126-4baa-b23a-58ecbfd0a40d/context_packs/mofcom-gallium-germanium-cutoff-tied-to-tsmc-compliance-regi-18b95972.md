## Your move

### MOFCOM Gallium-Germanium Cutoff Tied to TSMC Compliance Regime

D+1: MOFCOM announces 90-day export licensing freeze on gallium, germanium, refined rare-earth oxides, and tungsten to any firm 'participating in Taiwan Strait military activities,' with named entity list including TSMC Arizona, ASML Taiwan, Raytheon, Lockheed, BAE. Operative clock becomes the equity-market and semiconductor-supply-chain quarter, not the spring lift window.

#### Actions

- **T+1d** [PRC Ministry of Commerce (MOFCOM)] Publish export licensing freeze with named US/Taiwan/EU entity list under existing dual-use catalog authority
  - Target: Gallium, germanium, graphite, tungsten, 17 rare-earth oxides; ~140 named firms
  - Purpose: Trigger immediate Blue defense-industrial equity repricing and force Washington to choose between Taiwan posture and supply chain
- **T+3d** [State Administration of Foreign Exchange + PBOC] Quiet guidance to HKMA-cleared correspondent banks: Taiwan-exposed counterparties face enhanced settlement scrutiny
  - Target: CTBC, Cathay, Mega ICBC and their USD correspondent relationships
  - Purpose: Generate Taiwan banking-sector liquidity stress without formal sanctions
- **T+5d** [Cyberspace Administration of China + MIIT] Impose weekly compliance reporting on TSMC Nanjing fab regarding mainland staff travel, parts movements, and IP transfer
  - Target: TSMC Nanjing, UMC Xiamen, ASE Suzhou
  - Purpose: Convert TSMC's mainland footprint into a hostage that Taipei must defend politically while in cross-strait talks
- **T+7d** [State Council Taiwan Affairs Office] Announce 'Cross-Strait Industrial Stabilization Dialogue' offer: licensing freeze lifts upon Lai suspending referendum and entering 1992-framework talks
  - Target: Taipei, with industry-association back-channels to TSMC, Foxconn, MediaTek leadership
  - Purpose: Make Taiwan's semiconductor oligarchy the constituency lobbying Lai for accommodation
- **T+1d** [PLA Eastern Theater] Hold exercise baseline; no dispersal, no closures, no PLARF posture change
  - Target: All forces
  - Purpose: Deny Blue any kinetic predicate for Article V consultations or CSG forward sortie
- **T+2d** [MFA] Frame all measures as 'lawful regulatory response to Taiwan-authority militarization,' decline UN/WTO consultation requests as internal matter
  - Target: International audience, particularly Global South and EU industrial ministries
  - Purpose: Run the coercion on a regulatory-administrative clock Blue cannot answer at theater command

#### Intended effect

Replace the spring-2028 D-day clock with the equity-market quarter and the TSMC board-meeting cycle. Inflict measurable economic damage on Taiwan and Blue defense industrials within 30 days while presenting Blue zero kinetic predicate. Make Taiwan's own semiconductor oligarchy the lobby for accommodation.
