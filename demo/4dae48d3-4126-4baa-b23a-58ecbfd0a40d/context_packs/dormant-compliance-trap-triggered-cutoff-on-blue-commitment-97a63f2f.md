## Your move

### Dormant Compliance Trap: Triggered Cutoff On Blue Commitment

D+1 through D+30: No action announced. MOFCOM, PBOC, CAC quietly pre-position legal instruments — drafted but unsigned export-license suspensions, pre-cleared HKMA guidance memos, pre-staffed CAC inspection teams at TSMC Nanjing. Trigger condition is published as ambiguous regulatory language: instruments execute automatically only upon a defined Blue act (CSG-2 sortie west of 130°E, AIT advisor reinforcement, or Lai referendum scheduling). The coercion exists as a legal landmine, not an event. Beijing waits.

#### Actions

- **T+3d** [State Council legal affairs office] Quietly pre-stage and pre-coordinate the full MOFCOM/PBOC/CAC instrument package with auto-trigger conditions; brief select Western financial press off-record that 'measures are ready'
  - Target: Internal PRC regulatory apparatus; selective leak to FT/Bloomberg Beijing bureaus
  - Purpose: Create market knowledge that the cutoff is loaded and aimed without firing it
- **T+5d** [MFA spokesperson] Decline to confirm or deny the reported instrument package; refer questions to 'lawful regulatory authorities'
  - Target: International press
  - Purpose: Sustain ambiguity premium in Taiwan equity and TSMC supplier pricing
- **T+7d** [TAO back-channel] Communicate to TSMC, Foxconn, MediaTek boards that instruments execute on defined Blue/Taipei acts, with private list
  - Target: Taiwan industrial oligarchy
  - Purpose: Convert the oligarchy into a real-time lobby against any Lai or Washington escalatory step
- **T+1d** [PLA Eastern Theater] Hold exercise baseline; no posture change
  - Target: All forces
  - Purpose: Preserve the pure deferred-trigger character of the move

#### Intended effect

Blue and Taipei govern themselves under a sword they cannot see fall. Every Blue escalatory option carries an embedded supply-chain cost; risk-aversion in Washington and Taipei does Beijing's coercion for it, indefinitely, without Beijing executing.

#### Why a Red planner could justify this

Anchor: 2018–2024 'unreliable entity list' jurisprudence — the threat sustained leverage longer than the firings did. Serves goal 3 (no coalition crystallizes around an event that hasn't happened) and goal 2 (no kinetic or even regulatory predicate for Blue response). Relaxes Politburo constraint: no decision to execute is required, only a decision to load. Reversible at zero face-cost.

#### Convergence pattern this move breaks

Breaks the assumption that Red's opening move must be an *act* in the 7-day window. Red's opening move can be the credible *threat* of an act, with execution outsourced to Blue's own next decision.

#### Risks Red accepts

- Leak deemed bluff; market re-prices ambiguity to zero within 14 days
- Blue calls trigger by deliberately crossing it to force Red to execute or fold
- Taiwan industrial oligarchy lobbies Beijing rather than Taipei, inverting the intended pressure direction
